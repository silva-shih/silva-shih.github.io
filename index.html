Silva Shih

I am the data editor with FTChinese.com of Financial Times based in Beijing & Taipei, where I produce and oversee data-driven stories. Previously, I worked at Taiwan's business magazine CommonWealth (天下雜誌) and studied at NYU's Studio 20.

I am particularly interested in data-driven and cross-borders stories. Topics I follow include China’s censorship, foreign policy, economic influence, and Cross-Strait relations.

I taught data journalism workshops for the college/graduate students from time to time. I also co-founded and co-organize a non-profit community "Hacks/Hackers Taipei", where encourages techies and journos work together.
